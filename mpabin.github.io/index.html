LASERSHOT

Made for McGame Jam. Lasershot shrouds players in darkness, their only source of light being the lasers they shoot. Rounds can be frantic or sneaky, depending on how players play. Will you shoot at random, hoping to hit a player? Or will you hide in darkness until your opponent reveals themselves first?
Won best gameplay at McGame Jam.
  • 2 - 4 Players
  • 1 XBox Controller per player required

DO THE THING!

Made for Global Game Jam 2015 at the Montreal event. Do the Thing! is a fast-paced series of minigames. Players must work together to complete as many minigames they can. There are 8 minigames in total, with most of them featuring randomized player roles, so you have to keep an eye out for who has to do what!
Won judges' pick at GGJ Montreal.

FLAG IT

Flag It has two players simultaneously try to capture their opponent's flag while preventing their own from being captured. Each player has an unlimited supply of bouncy arrows. Power-ups (speed boost, shield, triple arrow) spawn on the board and can be picked up to gain an advantage. First to three wins!
  • 2 Players

STAMP OUT!!

Made for McHacks. Stamp Out!! has players fighting for a trophy while utilizing various weapons. On top of that, when a player steps on a tile, the tile changes to their color. The more tiles you have, the faster you move. Stamp Out!! requires both strategic and reflexive play to beat your opponent. It's a pretty intense game!
