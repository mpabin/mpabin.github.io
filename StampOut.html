Stamp Out! with Frank Brizuela

Project Description

This is a 2 player local multiplayer strategy game where you are trying to hold the golden carrot for a certain amount of time while controlling your territory. It's a war of attrition as both players constantly try to gain small advantages that might lead them to victory.

This game started out in a 24 hour hackathon where we got to prototype the concept.
