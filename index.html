Flag It! - with Frank Brizuela and Adam Kulidjian - Requires 2 Xbox Controllers 

This is a small 2D local multiplayer game that involves stealing your 
opponent's flag, shooting them down with arrows while dodging a bunch 
of enemies flying everywhere and collecting overpowered items to allow 
you to win!

The Last Fry - with Frank Brizuela - Requires 2 Xbox Controllers - for DATGAM-XI 

This was a game made for the MRGS DATGAM game jam. Imagine yourself 
eating a poutine with your friend until there is only one french fry 
left!! Certainly, you cannot let your friend have it. Therefore, you 
decide to fight for it. This is a small two player local multiplayer 
game where the players fight for the possession of the fry and then 
try to control it long enough to eat it completely.

Stamp Out!! - with Frank Brizuela - Coming out soon~

This is a 2 player local multiplayer strategy game where you are trying 
to hold the golden carrot for a certain amount of time while controlling 
your territory. It's a war of attrition as both players constantly try 
to gain small advantages that might lead them to victory.

This game started out in a 24 hour hackathon where we got to prototype 
the concept and as we found out how much depth this simple strategy game 
had, I decided to remake it.

Stamp Out!! (Prototype) - with Frank Brizuela

2D local multiplayer strategy game, created during a 24 hour Hackathon at 
McGill University.

Here are the rules:
-To win, you have to carry the trophy for 20 seconds in a row.
-The trophy appears some time after the game begins.
-When a player walks on a tile, the tile becomes inked with the player's 
color.
-When a player is holding the trophy, the player cannot color the field.
-The more tiles a player has, the faster they go and the slower they 
respawn.
-Players may shoot arrows to kill their opponents.
-Both players die when they run into each other.
